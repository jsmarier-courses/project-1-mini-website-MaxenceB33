Hello everyone, my name is Bahaban Maxence. I was born on March 4th 2003 in France, Gonesse (95). I lived most of my life in Gironde, South-West of France. I love basketball, I play basketball since I'm 5 years old. I also love reading, my first "big" readings is HP when I was in primary school.
